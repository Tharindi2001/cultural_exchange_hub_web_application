Virtual Cultural Tours

KANDY
Kandy is a major city in Sri Lanka located in the Central Province. It was the last capital of the ancient kings' era of Sri Lanka.
ANURADHAPURA
Anuradhapura is a major city in north central Sri Lanka. It is the capital city of North Central Province and one of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka.
SRI PADA
Sri Pada, also known as Adam's Peak, is a 2,243 m (7,359 ft) tall conical mountain located in central Sri Lanka. It is considered a sacred place by many religions.
